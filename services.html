Gasabo is a district (akarere) in Kigali city, the capital of Rwanda. The headquarters of Gasabo is located in Remera Sector. The district also includes large areas of the city itself, including Kacyiru, Kimironko, Remera, Nyarutarama, and Kimihurura.
